## Brief
This meeting should align stakeholders on current priorities, unblock critical execution items, and confirm ownership and timing for next actions.

## Agenda (30 minutes)
1. **Objective reset (5 min)**
   - Confirm the single outcome required from this meeting.
2. **Current-state snapshot (7 min)**
   - What is complete, in progress, blocked.
3. **Decision block (10 min)**
   - Resolve priority, resourcing, and timeline decisions.
4. **Risk and dependency check (5 min)**
   - Identify cross-team blockers and external constraints.
5. **Action lock (3 min)**
   - Owner, due date, next check-in.

## Decisions Needed
1. **Priority decision**
   - Which initiative is top priority for the next cycle?
2. **Scope decision**
   - What is explicitly in scope vs deferred?
3. **Resourcing decision**
   - Who owns execution and what capacity is allocated?
4. **Timeline decision**
   - What is the committed target date and checkpoint cadence?

## Open Questions
1. Which unresolved blocker currently has the highest impact?
2. What assumptions are we treating as facts without validation?
3. What dependency sits outside the team and who can clear it?
4. What would make this plan fail in the next 2 weeks?
5. What must be communicated immediately after this meeting, and by whom?
